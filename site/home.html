Why make it simple?

Because I genuinely do not care that much. As long as I get my wanted intent across and it is easy to read from raw code I will try my best at keeping simple.

As for the layout, I prefer to make it easy on every possible screen and device. Not every person is privledged enough to have a high-end computer or a large monitor, so I want to ensure that my website is accessible to as many people as possible.

Why not use a framework?

If I am not working on a professional project I see no reason to complicate things with a framework. Rather, making my own framework seems like a better project idea (and for my CV). Also because I do not know how to use any for these types of projects...
